The Story Of Our Band

Stage Fright is an up and coming rock band featuring many young artists from the suburbs of Ohio. We tour many venues in Cleveland, Columbus, Cincinnati, and many neighboring states. The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame has an immense connection to us. You can find our songs on all popular media outlets, including Spotify, Apple music, and YouTube
